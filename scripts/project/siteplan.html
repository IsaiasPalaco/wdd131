Site Plan for Mozambique Tourism Agency

Site Name

Travelers Sofa

This name was selected to reflect the adventurous spirit and the diverse experiences available in Mozambique. It aims to attract tourists looking to explore the natural beauty and cultural richness of the country.

Optional domain availability: travelersofa.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a comprehensive guide for tourists interested in exploring the diverse and beautiful landscapes of Mozambique. It will provide information on popular destinations, tour packages, travel tips, accommodation options, and more.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-visit destinations in Mozambique?
  • How can I book a tour package for a beach holiday?

Color Schema

Primary Color: darkgrey - Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: firebrick - Used for background highlights and buttons.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto - Used for body text.

Secondary Font: Antonr - Used for headings.
